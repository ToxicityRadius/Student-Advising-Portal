# Quick Start: Faculty Invitation System

## For Administrators

### Step 1: Access Manage Users
1. Login as admin
2. Click **"Manage Users"** in navigation

### Step 2: Send Invitation
1. Find **"Invite Faculty Member"** form at top of page
2. Enter faculty email (must end with @tip.edu.ph)
3. Select role:
   - **Adviser** - For student advisers
   - **Admin** - For program chairs
4. Click **"Send Invitation"**

### Step 3: Confirmation
- ✅ Success message appears
- Faculty receives professional email invitation
- Invitation valid for 48 hours

---

## For Faculty Members

### Step 1: Check Email
- Look for email: "Invitation to Join Student Advising System"
- Check spam folder if not in inbox

### Step 2: Click Invitation Link
- Click **"Accept Invitation & Create Account"** button
- Or copy/paste the link in browser

### Step 3: Complete Registration
1. Your email and role are pre-filled
2. Enter your **First Name**
3. Enter your **Last Name**
4. Create a **Password** (minimum 6 characters)
5. **Confirm Password**
6. Click **"Complete Registration"**

### Step 4: Start Using System
- You're automatically logged in
- Full access to your role's features
- No email verification needed!

---

## Important Notes

⏰ **Invitation expires in 48 hours** - Complete registration promptly

🔒 **Role is pre-assigned** - You cannot change your role during registration

📧 **Email must be @tip.edu.ph** - Other domains are not accepted

✅ **No activation needed** - Your account is immediately active

❌ **Link works once only** - Cannot reuse the same invitation link

---

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid or expired invitation link"
**Problem:** Link doesn't work  
**Solution:** 
- Check if 48 hours have passed
- Ask admin to resend invitation
- Make sure you're using the full link from email

### "An account with this email already exists"
**Problem:** Cannot complete registration  
**Solution:** 
- Try logging in with existing account
- Contact admin if you forgot password
- Use password reset if needed

### Email not received
**Problem:** No invitation email  
**Solution:** 
- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait 5-10 minutes for delivery
- Ask admin to resend invitation
- Verify correct email address was used

### "Password must be at least 6 characters"
**Problem:** Cannot submit form  
**Solution:** 
- Use longer password
- Mix letters and numbers recommended
- No spaces in password
